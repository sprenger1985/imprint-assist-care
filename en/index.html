Privacy Policy AssistCare

Protecting your privacy is very important to us. This app can generally be used without providing any personal data.

1. Controller

The controller responsible for data processing within the meaning of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is:

Sebastian Sprenger acting under the developer name “Azubi Assist Care”

Email: info@azubi-assist-care.com

2. Collection and Processing of Data

a) No Personal Data

This app does not collect or process any personal data. In particular, no names, email addresses, IP addresses, location data, or other information that could identify individual users are processed.

b) Anonymous Diagnostic Data

To ensure technical stability and to improve the app, only anonymous diagnostic data is processed.

This data:

  • cannot be assigned to a specific user
  • does not contain any personal information
  • does not allow any conclusions to be drawn about the user’s identity

3. Purpose of Data Processing

The anonymous diagnostic data is processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Error analysis and troubleshooting
  • Ensuring the technical functionality of the app
  • Improving the app

4. Legal Basis

As no personal data is processed, the GDPR applies only to a limited extent. Where a legal basis is required, processing is carried out on the basis of Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in the technical optimization of the app).

5. Data Sharing

The collected data is not shared with third parties.

6. Storage and Deletion

Anonymous diagnostic data is stored only for as long as necessary for the purposes stated and is then deleted.

7. User Rights

Since no personal data is processed, there are no rights to access, rectification, deletion, or data portability under the GDPR.

If you have any questions about data protection, you may contact us at any time using the contact details provided above.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to amend this privacy policy if necessary due to technical or legal developments.
